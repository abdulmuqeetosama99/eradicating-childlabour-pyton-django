Child labour refers to the exploitation of children through any form of work that deprives children of their childhood, interferes with their ability to attend regular school, and is mentally, physically, socially or morally harmful.

This web application will help in eradicating CHILD LABOUR from the nation and making the society free from this violation on childrens. It will build an educated nation and the nation's economy will grow.

More about the working of Application

1. What is Citizen module


In this citizen can report the details about the children and see the status of the children or any interested parents or children can also give the child details to give the child a bright future.

2. What is volunteer module


In this volunteer will receive the details and location of child.He will verify the child details and will notify whether that child is actually needy or not.

3. What is NGO module


In this NGO will be notified with the details of needy child and admit in his NGO and provide education and all facilities.

4. What is Staff module


Staff will take care of all the database and user activities. It can have all rights of admin.
